Instructions

Concentration, also know as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply Paris, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid fasce down on a surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

Try to get the highest score you can!
